KTM X-BOW REVIEW

Once behind the wheel, the first thing that stands out is how tractable the X-Bow Comp R is, even at low speeds. A tilt-and-telescoping wheel and a sliding pedal assembly make it easy for the driver to make adjustments. The clutch is nicely weighted, and the short-throw six-speed manual shifter moves through gears with a deliberate confidence.

THE FERRARI F8 TRIBUTO

At first glance, the F8 Tributo seems to be a direct evolution of the 488 GTB -- one that incorporates many of the performance advancements found on the 488 Pista we drove last summer. It’s built around a 3.9-liter turbocharged 90-degree V8 that produces 720 cv, or 710 hp, at 8,000 and 568 lb-ft of torque at 3,250 rpm (figures virtually identical to those of the Pista). Ferrari has not disclosed the transmission, but we suspect it will be a version of the seven-speed dual-clutch found on the F8 Tributo’s predecessors.

KIA'S GENEVA CONCEPT

One of the most intriguing concept cars headed to the Geneva motor show in a matter of days is what could be a preview of Kia's first electric sedan, which could follow the automaker's e-Niro electric crossover and Soul EV hatch. Kia has finally confirmed that the concept will be a four-door, one that will likely adopt a coupe-like profile.
